Placeholders

Each template is able to insert information by placeholders. Placeholders are noted by this syntax: 

[#placeholder#]

List of available placeholders:

nr
Internal number of newsletter.
title
Mail title of newsletter.
content
Content of newsletter.
prefix
Saluation depending on given gender of reader.
firstname
Firstname of reader.
surname
Surname of reader.
registerDate
Date of newsletter registration.
registerTime
Time of newsletter registration.
linkUnregister
Link to unregister.
linkView
Link to view in browser.
tracking
Invisible image to track opening of mail.

Example

Within this example 3 placeholders will be inserted: 

Hello [#prefix#] [#firstname#] [#surname#]!
The resulting mail to a male reader will contain like: 
Hello Mr. John Doe!
Having a female reader it would be like; 
Hello Mrs. Jane Doe!
Without gender information (or transgender) would be like: 
Hello Jordan Doe!
